About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Researcher with strong research interests in the opportunities provided by Wearable Technology in fields of Sleep & Circadian Biology, Neuroscience and Cognition. My work leverages Statistical & Machine Learning techniques for Time Series data modelling and analysis to uncover insights into human health and rhythms.

Apart from a strong background in modelling time series, I have experience with handling large data sets, experimental clinical studies as well as years of experience in the multidisciplinary research (involving stakeholders from academia, industry, and the hospitals).

I aim to bridge the gap between biological insights and computational methods, developing robust tools for analyzing complex physiological data from wearables.
